CarSite – Professional Automotive Ecommerce Platform
1. Project Overview

CarSite is a comprehensive, enterprise-grade automotive ecommerce web application designed to facilitate the professional listing, browsing, management, and sale of motor vehicles.

The platform is built to industry standards and focuses on:

High-quality vehicle presentation

Structured and comprehensive vehicle data

Professional UI/UX

Lead generation and CRM functionality

Scalable backend architecture

Administrative control and analytics

The system supports both public users (buyers) and internal administrators (management and sales teams), offering a seamless digital vehicle marketplace experience.

2. Background & Purpose

The automotive market has increasingly shifted toward digital discovery and online engagement. Modern car buyers expect:

Detailed vehicle information

High-quality imagery

Transparent pricing

Quick contact options (WhatsApp, phone, email)

Easy filtering and search capabilities

Mobile-first experience

CarSite is designed to meet these expectations while also providing dealership-level inventory management, analytics, and operational control through a robust admin panel.

The goal is to deliver a professional automotive ecommerce experience comparable to established industry platforms.

7. Technology Stack

CarSite is built using modern, scalable technologies:

Frontend & Backend -Next.js (App Router) Serverless architecture

Database & Authentication - Supabase (PostgreSQL)., Supabase Auth with role-based access control

Media Storage - Cloudinary (image hosting and optimization)

Hosting & Deployment  - Vercel (serverless deployment)

This stack ensures:

High performance

Scalability

Security

Maintainability

Production-grade deployment readiness
